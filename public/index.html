Opinion Essay

Electronic Devices in Classrooms

Why schools should teach responsible use instead of applying strict bans.

My Opinion

I believe schools should not strictly restrict electronic devices in classrooms.

First, smartphones can be useful learning tools when used properly during lessons.

Second, banning devices removes opportunities for students to access information and educational apps quickly.

Also, strict bans can make students hide usage or miss chances to learn digital responsibility.

Instead of outright bans, schools should teach responsible use and set reasonable rules.

For these reasons, I think limiting devices completely is the wrong approach.
